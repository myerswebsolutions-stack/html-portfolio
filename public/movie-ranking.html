The Best Movies According to Dustin

My top 3 movies of all time.

Garden State

This movie follows a young man who returns to his New Jersey hometown after his mother’s death and reconnects with old friends and a quirky girl who helps him confront his emotional numbness. It’s a quiet, offbeat coming-of-age story about healing and belonging.

It's a Wonderful Life

This wonderful Christmas movie follows George Bailey, who, feeling hopeless, is shown by an angel what the world would be like if he had never existed. He realizes the profound impact he’s had on others and rediscovers the value of his own life.

Star Wars: A New Hope

This popular sci-fi fantasy movie follows Luke Skywalker as he joins rebels to stop the evil Empire and destroy the Death Star. Along the way, he discovers his destiny and the power of the Force.
